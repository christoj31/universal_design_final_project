About

Shedeur Deion Sanders (born February 7, 2002) is an American professional football quarterback. He played college football for Jackson State and Colorado and was selected in the 2025 NFL Draft.

Early life

Sanders was born on February 7, 2002, in Tyler, Texas, to Deion and Pilar Sanders. He attended Trinity Christian School in Cedar Hill, Texas, where his father served as offensive coordinator. As a senior in high school, he completed 251 of 366 pass attempts for 3,702 yards and 43 touchdowns. He was rated a four-star prospect and initially committed to Florida Atlantic before flipping to Jackson State after his father was named head coach.

College career

Jackson State

Sanders enrolled at Jackson State in January 2021 and was named the Tigers' starting quarterback for the 2021 fall season. In his freshman and sophomore seasons he posted strong passing numbers, won the Jerry Rice Award as the most outstanding freshman in the FCS, and received SWAC honors. He also signed name, image, and likeness (NIL) deals with companies such as Gatorade and Nike.

Colorado

After his father Deion Sanders was hired at Colorado, Shedeur transferred and became the Buffaloes' starting quarterback. In his first game he set a school record with 510 passing yards. He missed the final game of the 2023 season with a back injury but returned to lead Colorado to a 9–4 record in 2024, earning Big 12 Offensive Player of the Year and the Johnny Unitas Golden Arm Award.

Professional career

On April 26, 2025, Sanders was selected in the fifth round (144th overall) by the Cleveland Browns in the 2025 NFL Draft. He signed a four-year contract with the Browns and joined the team as a rookie. Media coverage during and after the draft included various analyses and some critical scouting remarks; the league later investigated an incident involving a prank call to Sanders during the draft process.

Personal life

Sanders is the son of Pro Football Hall of Fame cornerback Deion Sanders and the younger brother of safety Shilo Sanders. He has four siblings and his parents are divorced.
